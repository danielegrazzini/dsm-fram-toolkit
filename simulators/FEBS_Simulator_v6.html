OPERATIONAL FREQUENCY — freqi / r How many times per cycle did each function trigger variability propagation?
freqi source:
Simulated: freqi is estimated from the Monte Carlo runs (standard mode, freq≈0 if Pbase is low). Empirical: you provide measured values from incident logs or direct observation.
Interpretation of freqi/r:   <0.05 = rare (structural IRR dominates)  |  0.05–0.20 = moderate (dynamic component relevant)  |  >0.20 = frequent (dynamic IRR may rerank functions)

Formula effect:   IRRi = [Σ Sij · IMPACTij · ICi · MLVi] · (1 + freqi/r)  — when freqi/r = 0, IRR reduces to its structural approximation (paper Tables 5 & 6).
